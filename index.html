Find us

Our distribution network spans over 4,400 points across Canada, the United States, and Mexico, including major retail chains like Metro, Loblaws, and Wal-Mart, among many others. This extensive presence allows us to provide a consistent and reliable supply of fresh agricultural products to our valued customers.

Our products are harvested in Hidalgo, Mexico, by a community of farmers who carry pride and tradition in every furrow of the land. It is these farmers who make it possible for us to bring exceptional vegetables to your table. We are filled with joy knowing that we contribute to nourishing families with high-quality and fresh produce. We are Can-Mex, and we are committed to excellence at every stage of the process, from planting to your table. Thank you for making us a part of your life.
